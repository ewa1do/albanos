Who we are

Welcome to Albano's Pub and Pizza restaurant, Covington's newest neighborhood watering hole and pizza destination.

My name is Aleksandra King and I'm the owner of the restaurant, after a series of wonderful and sometimes difficult years of my life, I decided to pursue my dream and become a chef by profession.

There are so many things I would like to tell you, so I would like to start with the basics.

Why I chose food as a career?

I always followed my heart, and that worked out for me. I knew what I needed to be happy, and didn't mind being a little strange.

Being this way helped me find what I needed to fit into my life, and I tried to be okay with who I was, even if other people found it difficult.
